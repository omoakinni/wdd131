HealthyDiet Site Project

Author: Akinlolu Tomiwa Ariyo

Overview

HealthyDiet is a website designed to showcase an array of nutritious meals, aloowing users to sort through them, it also gives detailed hints about the products offered by a food ordering company dedicated to promoting healthy eating.

Purpose

This project was chosen to help small business owners with a website to showcase some of their products, a user-friendly platform that promotes healthy eating.

Audience

Our target audience is everyone age 18 and above, we make ordering healthy meals easy for all
